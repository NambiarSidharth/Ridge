ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Why are we doing this?

Many students in a college are unaware of the project works going on in their departments and the research activities undergoing in the college. The main reason for this can be communication gap between the students or between students and professors.

Vision

We, here as developers try to solve this problem by creating a platform where ongoing projects of the departments will be listed. Any students interested to work on the listed projects can contact the guide or the head of the project.

Motivation

The problems listed are the problem faced by almost every developer including us. Searching for a solution resulted in non-satisfactory results. So, we decided to take up the problem and solve it on our own.
